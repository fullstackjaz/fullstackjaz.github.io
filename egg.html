ABOUT US

Co-founded by Jaz Syed and Olive Ahamzie, Eggcellent represents the UK's most premier egg restaurant, showcasing the versatility and deliciousness of eggs in all their forms, from classic omelettes and egg sandwiches to more unique creations like our egg and avocado toast or our smoked salmon and scrambled eggs

All of our ingredients are freshly sourced in the UK and all of our eggs derive from happy, free range hens. We are true believers that all chickens (and all animals for that matter!) deserve a high quality standard to living. We also believe that this returns a more delicious egg!
